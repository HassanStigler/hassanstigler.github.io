GoFileRoom Platform Turnaround

GoFileRoom is a tax and accounting platform serving enterprise firms across the United States. When I took ownership of the product, it was generating roughly $40M in annual revenue with very little ongoing investment. Customers trusted it to run their firms — but they did not trust that the product had a future.

The Situation

Firms depended on GoFileRoom for mission-critical document workflows, yet lacked confidence that their needs were being heard or that meaningful improvements were coming. Integration, automation, and operational visibility lagged behind customer expectations.

Internally, this created strategic risk. Despite strong revenue and deep customer trust, some believed the product would not survive the next three years without modernization and a clear platform vision.

The gap wasn’t value — it was leadership, visibility, and a credible roadmap for the future.

My Mandate

I was brought in to give GoFileRoom a real product future: restore customer confidence, modernize the platform, and create a foundation leadership could confidently invest in.

What I Led

  • Created a platform roadmap aligned to revenue, retention, and customer trust
  • Built public APIs and webhook-based workflows to enable automation and integration
  • Led CIAM migration to a new identity platform
  • Drove SOC 2 readiness and security modernization in 2025
  • Built telemetry, dashboards, and escalation paths for operational visibility
  • Managed and aligned development analysts and engineering execution to product priorities
  • Built direct relationships with enterprise power users to shape roadmap decisions

What Changed

  • $38M → $43M in platform revenue
  • –15 → +3 pNPS turnaround
  • Support teams gained visibility and faster response times
  • Customers adopted APIs to replace manual staff workflows
  • Leadership regained confidence in the product’s future

Why Leadership Invested

By restoring customer trust, delivering real platform improvements, and creating transparency through telemetry and roadmaps, GoFileRoom shifted from a profitable but neglected system into a modern, investable platform.

Leadership didn’t reinvest because revenue was collapsing — they reinvested because the product finally had a credible future.

Why It Worked

This wasn’t a single project or an API release. It was an operating-model change.

Product, engineering, security, support, and customers were aligned around a simple truth: trust comes from reliability, visibility, and the ability to integrate.

APIs, dashboards, identity, and customer relationships were the levers that made that possible.
